Term Loan

It’s a normal home loan products which is offered by every banks in which there is a specific time period in which the borrower have to pay regular emi.

Maxgain

It is same as term loan but the additional feature of this product is that it works as a overdraft facility. In this products borrower can put their extra funds in home loan account and can save a lot on interest part. Whenever borrower feels to take out their extra funds they can withdraw.

Privilege

This products is same as term loan but specially designed for the government employee with increased loan tenure upto 75 years and this helps in increasing their eligibility.

Shaurya

This is also same as term loan but designed for Young generation in which their eligibility is increased.
